Description

This image was taken with iTelescope. Rather than taking with the image in color, the image was captured narow band filters (Hydrogen alpha, Sulphur and Oxygen) and the images mapped to color using the Hubble Color Palette – the sulphur signal was mapped to red, hydrogen alpa to red and oxygen to blue.

Equipment

iTelescope T121
  • Telescope:Planewave 17" CDK
  • CCD: FLI-PL6303E
  • Mount: Planewave Ascension 200HR -
  • Filters: Astrodon 5nm Ha, SII and OIII

Location

Mayhill, New Mexico, USA

Date

April / May 2016

Data

Final data consisted of:

  • Ha: 6 * 10 mins
  • OIII: 6 * 10 mins at bin 2
  • SII: 6 * 10 mins at bin 2

Processing notes

Processing of the data was achieved with PixInsight, LightRoom and Photoshop. As the sulphur and oxygen signals were weak, the signals were 'boosted' using software binning (summing groups of pixels). Assignment of colors done with Hubble Color Palette: SII mapped to red, Ha mapped to Green and OIII mapped to blue.

  • FixFITS: remove bright vertical line which were faults in the CCD.
  • PixInsight: Cosmetic Correction, Star Alignnment, Image Integration, RGB combination, stretching, Noise Reduction using ACDNR.
  • LightRoom: Color and level adjustment.
